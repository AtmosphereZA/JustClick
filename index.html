About JustClick

For the past 20 years, JustClick has been active in more than 45 schools and nursery schools in the east of Pretoria, providing unique perceptual stimulation for preschoolers.

What We Offer:

  • Unique perceptual stimulation using laptops and tablets as educational tools
  • New and exciting program every week (updated annually)
  • Individual attention with only 5 learners per class
  • Age-appropriate software and graded lesson plans
  • Weekly evaluation according to occupational therapist standards
  • Early detection of possible problems
  • Three comprehensive reports per year
  • Regular feedback to teachers
  • Affordable fees

🎯 Our Mission

To develop each toddler in a positive way by ensuring that each lesson is an unforgettable experience while emphasizing each child's uniqueness and individuality.

Why is JustClick Different?

Technology is here to stay, and it is not just educational – it is FUN too!

🚀 Our Approach

  • Devices used primarily to instill fundamental skills for reading and maths
  • Skills developed incidentally in the learning programme
  • Age-specific, detailed, educational, and measurable programmes
  • Computers and tablets as educational apparatus in discovery journey
  • Knowledgeable presenters provide loving, attentive guidance
  • Foundation for Robotics education

What is STEM Education?

STEM education stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. It emphasizes the integration of these disciplines to provide a holistic and interdisciplinary approach to learning, promoting hands-on, experiential learning and encouraging inquiry-based methods.

⚠️ When Technology Becomes Detrimental:

  • When electronic devices become just pleasurable pastimes
  • When toddlers repeatedly play the same games and become intellectually lazy
  • When toddlers spend more than 30 minutes per day on devices
